Student Fee Management (Bakti Idhata)

Product Management Requirement Analysis QA Coordination

Overview

This project serves as a bridge between developers and users, ensuring clear communication and alignment throughout the development process. It focuses on building requirements tailored to student fee (tuition fee) management needs for the Bakti Idhata Foundation, covering both functional and business aspects.

Key Responsibilities

  • Gathering and defining product requirements based on user and stakeholder needs.
  • Translating user needs into clear, structured, and actionable documentation.
  • Managing development timelines and coordinating deliverables.
  • Supporting and coordinating application testing to ensure product quality and readiness for release.
  • Ensuring the final product meets user expectations and business objectives.

Technical Details

The role focused on the lifecycle management of the software, bridging the gap between technical implementation and business requirements to ensure a successful deployment of the fee management system.
