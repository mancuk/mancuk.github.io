Netverse

Android Video Streaming Loyalty System

Overview

Netverse is a streaming platform by PT. Net Media Digital. I was involved in the maintenance and development of new features to enhance user engagement and viewing experience.

Key Contributions

  • Implemented new features including Games and Loyalty programs
  • Developed "Next Episode" functionality for seamless binge-watching
  • Updated app appearance and UI improvements
  • Optimized and updated the video player for better performance
  • General maintenance and bug fixing

Technical Details

The project involved working with complex video streaming architectures, integrating third-party SDKs for games and analytics, and ensuring a smooth user experience across various Android devices.
