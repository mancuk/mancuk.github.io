Oyes (Aplikasi Mobile Penjualan Dagangan Pasar)

Android Java/Kotlin Payment Gateway

Overview

Android App for market seller in PD Pasar Jaya for order, pay and send the product from Jakgrosir PD Pasar Jaya to purchaser. Spending amount of shopping can be process via virtual number account (VA) that facilitated by PT Bank Mandiri Tbk. Product distribution for this program assist with PT. Tiki JNE that manage product from Jakgrosir PD Pasar Jaya agency to trader in PD Pasar Jaya.

This App facilitated with payment fiture also product repurchase (PPOB) that promote and increase sellers activity.

Key Features

  • Integration with Jakgrosir PD Pasar Jaya inventory
  • Virtual Account (VA) payments via PT Bank Mandiri Tbk
  • Delivery tracking integration with PT. Tiki JNE
  • PPOB (Payment Point Online Bank) features for additional revenue
  • User-friendly interface for market sellers

Technical Details

The app is developed for the Android platform, utilizing secure payment gateway integrations and real-time logistics tracking APIs. It focuses on reliability and ease of use for traders in the PD Pasar Jaya ecosystem.
