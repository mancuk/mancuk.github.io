IoT & Electrical Support (Apps Reward)

IoT Electrical Engineering System Integration

Overview

This project involved supporting the IoT and electrical infrastructure for an apps reward system. The role focused on the physical assembly, integration, and maintenance of hardware components to ensure seamless operation with the software ecosystem.

Key Responsibilities

  • Build and assemble IoT hardware components, including sensors, controllers, and electrical modules.
  • Integrate software and hardware systems to ensure reliable device communication and data exchange.
  • Configure, deploy, and maintain IoT devices in operational environments.
  • Perform application and system testing to validate functionality, performance, and stability.
  • Troubleshoot hardware, firmware, and electrical issues.
  • Support system calibration, upgrades, and maintenance activities.
  • Collaborate with software developers and engineers during integration and testing phases.

Technical Impact

Ensured the reliability and stability of the IoT network, enabling accurate data tracking and reward distribution. The work bridged the gap between physical devices and the digital reward application.
