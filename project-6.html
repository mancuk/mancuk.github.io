SPMB Bakti Idhata

Web Platform Education Digitalization

Overview

The product is designed to support the SPMB (New Student Registration) program for Bakti Idhata Foundation, providing a digital platform for managing the end-to-end new student registration process. It streamlines student enrollment by enabling online registration, data collection, and administrative management.

Key Benefits

  • Manage student registration data efficiently and securely.
  • Simplify the application and selection process.
  • Reduce manual paperwork and operational errors.
  • Improve transparency and accessibility for students and parents.

Impact

This platform ensures a smooth, reliable, and scalable registration experience for both administrators and applicants, modernizing the enrollment process for the educational institution.
