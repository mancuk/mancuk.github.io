Epaper Bisnis Indonesia

Android Digital Publishing News
Play Store

Overview

Bisnis Indonesia, The largest business and economic daily newspaper in Indonesia. This apps will give you new experience of reading Bisnis Indonesia Newspaper printed product much easier and faster, wherever and whenever.

Key Features

  • Digital replica of the printed newspaper
  • Easy navigation and reading modes
  • News feed integration
  • Subscription management
  • User-friendly interface for reading on mobile

Technical Details

This application focuses on delivering high-quality digital reading experiences. It includes features for rendering newspaper layouts efficiently on mobile devices, ensuring text is readable and navigation is intuitive.
